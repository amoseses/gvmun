GVMUNC X - Conference Details

Debates. Ideas. Solutions.

Greetings international delegates,

Welcome to the tenth Global Virtual Model United Nations Conference (GVMUNC), hosted on October 19th! Join us for the day as we introduce ideas, debate solutions, and finally implement resolutions. Participate in a growing international committee of diplomacy and discussion, and prove your skills as a Model UN delegate.

Committees Available

For this iteration, GVMUN is offering delegates the option between two different committees, ranging from beginner to advanced. They are as follows:

United Nations International Children's Emergency Fund (UNICEF)

Beginner

Delegates in this committee will represent all UN member states, and will address the global rise of malnutrition among children, or combat child labor in the industrial sector.

Futuristic Crisis: Water Wars

Advanced

This committee is set in the near future, 2040, when chronic water shortages begin to cause widespread conflict between nations in a fight for survival. Delegates will be the leaders of all current UN member states.

Awards

Each committee will have three awards, as follows:

  • Best Delegate - 1
  • Outstanding Delegate - 1
  • Best Research and Preparation - 1

Note that a position paper, while not required, is strongly suggested for award eligibility. Delegates must research their topic thoroughly, starting with the provided topic briefs, and prepare for the committee. Additionally, make sure to check the code of conduct, and reach out to us if you have any questions. The email is: gvmunsecretariat@gmail.com

Additionally, please follow our Instagram @gvmun_official to support us and receive updates.

We hope to see you on October 19th for a day of collaboration, debate, and solutions.

Your Secretary-Generals,

Kush Dave and Ayushmaan Mukherjee

GVMUNEC VI 2025 - Essay Competition

Debates. Ideas. Solutions.

Applications

Delegates: Oct 18, 2025 - Dec 21, 2025

Head Delegates: Oct 18, 2025 - Dec 21, 2025

Faculty Advisor: Oct 18, 2025 - Dec 21, 2025

International delegates,

GVMUN is pleased to announce the sixth iteration of the Global Virtual Model United Nations Essay Competition (GVMUNEC), a contest for aspiring writers and thinkers to share their insight on the world. The competition will be composed of essay submissions from an informed international audience looking to spark change in the world. The ideas in each essay should bring a fresh, new perspective to international relations and policymaking.

Competitors will be able to choose between two prompts, as follows:

Topic 1

How can nations restructure global supply chains to balance economic resilience with efficiency in an era of geopolitical fragmentation?

Topic 2

How should the international community regulate the global AI economy to promote innovation while preventing inequality and monopolization?

Requirements:

They will need to write and submit a 600-800 word essay answering one of the prompts. Submission applications will be open on September 15th, 2025. Please attach your essay as a PDF under the position paper section with the following title format: [Name] - [Topic Number] - [Word Count]. All essays should use a Times New Roman size 12 font. Essays that do not meet all of the previous requirements will not be considered.

A works cited page is highly recommended, but not required, unless a paper has citations, in which case a works cited becomes mandatory.

December 21st is the deadline, although participants are strongly encouraged to submit their essays as early as they can.

Awards & Recognition:

After the submissions have been reviewed, the top two essays from each category will receive recognition through awards and posts on our social media platforms, such as Instagram and LinkedIn. Winners will also gain the opportunity to become GVMUN Interns to work with our team.

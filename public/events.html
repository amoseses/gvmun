Greetings, international delegates,

Welcome to the Fourteenth Global Virtual Model United Nations Conference (GVMUNC XIV), hosted on August 15, 2026, at 8:30 AM EST and based in Bridgewater, New Jersey! Join us for the day as we introduce ideas, debate solutions, and finally implement resolutions. Participate in a growing international committee of diplomacy and discussion, and prove your skills as a Model UN delegate.

Committees Available

For this iteration, GVMUN is offering delegates three committee options, ranging from beginner to advanced. They are as follows:

United Nations Development Programme (UNDP)

Beginner

Delegates will represent UN member states and debate Financing the SDG Recovery and Harnessing Digital Transformation as a Catalyst for Inclusive Human Development.

Historical Crisis Committee (HCC) Eurozone Crisis

Advanced

This committee is set in 2010, and delegates will assume control of various nations as they navigate the Eurozone Crisis in a timeline where it goes global.

International Press

Advanced

Press delegates will report on conference proceedings, interview participants, and publish coverage of committee developments throughout the event.

Awards

Each committee will have three awards, as follows:

  • Best Delegate - 1
  • Outstanding Delegate - 1
  • Best Research and Preparation - 1

Note that a position paper, while not required, is strongly suggested for award eligibility. Delegates must research their topic thoroughly and prepare for the committee. Additionally, make sure to check the UNA-USA rules of procedure (RoP), and reach out to us if you have any questions. Our email is: secretariat@gvmun.com

Additionally, please follow our Instagram @gvmun_official to support us and receive updates.

We hope to see you on August 15th for a day of collaboration, debate, and solutions.

Regards,

Ayushmaan Mukherjee and Jackson Dorris

Secretary-Generals, GVMUN

Applications

Delegates: Open now - Jun 26, 2026

Submission Deadline: Jun 26, 2026

Format: Free, fully virtual essay competition

International delegates,

GVMUN is pleased to announce the ninth iteration of the Global Virtual Model United Nations Essay Competition (GVMUNEC IX), a contest for aspiring writers and thinkers to share their insights on the world. The competition will consist of essay submissions from an informed international audience seeking to spark change in the world. The ideas in each essay should bring a fresh, new perspective to international relations and policymaking.

Finally, please follow our Instagram @gvmun_official, and reach out to secretariat@gvmun.com with any questions.

We're looking forward to reading your submissions and reviewing your ideas,

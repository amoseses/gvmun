August 8, 2025 UN Engagement

International Youth Day '25 at the UN: GVMUN Team Participates in UN Secretariat Building Event

The GVMUN team recently attended International Youth Day - hosted by AFS, UNITAR, and the Mission of Malta to the UN - at the UN Secretariat Building in NYC. The event focused on how young people are advancing multilateral cooperation through technology and partnerships.

Key Highlights:

  • GVMUN delegates participated in sessions inside the UN complex, including time in the ECOSOC Chamber
  • Engaged with UN officials, diplomats, and youth leaders on policy, education, and technology
  • Event featured speakers including Felipe Paullier (Assistant Secretary-General for Youth Development), Samim Hamidy (UNITAR), and Daniel Obst (AFS)
  • Marks GVMUN's pivot toward greater policy impact and youth-led initiatives

"International Youth Day reminded us that policy is most powerful when those it affects help shape it. We're doubling down on programs that turn youth perspectives into concrete proposals and partnerships."

— Kush Dave & Ayushmaan Mukherjee, GVMUN Secretary-Generals

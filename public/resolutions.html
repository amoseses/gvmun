What Are Resolutions?

Resolutions are formal documents that outline comprehensive solutions to global challenges discussed in Model UN committees. They represent the collaborative effort of delegates working together to address pressing international issues through diplomatic consensus and innovative policy proposals.

Each resolution contains operative clauses that detail specific actions, recommendations, and frameworks for addressing the topic at hand. Our collection showcases the exceptional work produced by GVMUN delegates across various committees and conferences.

Resolution GVMUNC VIII - 2024

Resolution on Humanitarian Aid and Reconstruction in Ukraine

United Nations Security Council

This resolution addresses the ongoing humanitarian crisis in Ukraine, establishing frameworks for international aid delivery, refugee assistance, and post-conflict reconstruction efforts. The document outlines specific mechanisms for coordinating humanitarian relief while respecting sovereignty and promoting sustainable recovery. Passed with broad consensus, it demonstrates effective multilateral diplomacy in crisis response.

Resolution GVMUNC VII - 2024

Comprehensive Framework for Climate Action and Green Technology Transfer

United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP)

This groundbreaking resolution establishes a comprehensive framework for accelerating climate action through enhanced international cooperation on green technology transfer. The document outlines mechanisms for developing nations to access clean energy technologies, financing instruments for climate adaptation, and accountability measures for emission reduction commitments under the Paris Agreement.

Resolution GVMUNC VI - 2023

International Cybersecurity Cooperation and Digital Rights Protection

United Nations General Assembly Third Committee

This resolution addresses the growing threats in cyberspace while balancing security concerns with digital rights protection. It establishes international norms for state behavior in cyberspace, creates mechanisms for information sharing on cyber threats, and promotes capacity building for developing nations. The resolution emphasizes the importance of protecting privacy and freedom of expression in the digital age.

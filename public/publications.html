Publication Formatting Help

Article Submissions

Please send a title, author name, 2-3 sentence excerpt, category, preferred publication date, image or image idea, and the complete article text with hyperlinks or citations clearly marked.

Research Papers

Please send the PDF, title, authors, publication month, abstract, keywords, citation details, and any external links such as SSRN so the research page can list the paper consistently.

Assets

High-resolution images, author headshots, partner logos, and alt text help us publish faster and keep each page accessible and visually consistent.

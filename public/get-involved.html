Ways to Get Involved

Internships

Join our dynamic internship program and gain hands-on experience in international relations, event management, and diplomatic engagement. Our interns work directly with the secretariat on conference planning, research, and outreach initiatives.

  • Direct mentorship from experienced team members
  • Certificate of completion
  • Networking opportunities with global delegates
  • Volunteer hours

Duration: Fall 2025

Commitment: 3-5 hours per week

Requirements: High school or university students

Apply for Fall Internship

Fellowship Program

Our prestigious fellowship program is designed for exceptional students who want to become involved in policy analysis and global advocacy. Fellows receive mentorship to ultimately write a 10-15 page policy paper over the course of eight weeks.

  • Lead research projects and policy initiatives
  • Attend special workshops
  • Publish research to journals through SSRN
  • Access to exclusive research mentorship

Duration: Fall 2025

Commitment: 5-10 hours per week

Requirements: Interest in policy and global issues

Apply for Fall Fellowship

Join the Secretariat

Become a core member of the GVMUN team and help shape the future of virtual Model UN. Secretariat members have significant responsibility in conference planning, policy development, and organizational strategy.

  • Core decision-making responsibilities
  • Lead conference committees and sessions
  • Develop organizational policies and procedures
  • Represent GVMUN at external events

Duration: 1-year commitment

Commitment: 10+ hours per week

Requirements: Extensive MUN and leadership background required

What We Offer

Training

We will offer live training, videos, and documents - all free of charge - to those new to MUN.

Political Education

We hope to politically educate our delegates in the democratic process, the UN, different forms of governments, and more.

Media

We will strive to be an unbiased source of news on key international events, especially regarding the UN.

Impact

We hope to offer mentorship and essential resources to delegates to spark change in their communities.

Partnerships

To expand our reach and improve quality, we'll secure partnerships with local clubs, organizations, and eventually, UN bodies

Resolutionmaking

Conferences will be productive and impactful through our improved resolutionmaking procedures.
